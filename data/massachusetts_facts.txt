Massachusetts is one of the original 13 colonies of the United States and is located in the New England region.
Boston is the capital and largest city of Massachusetts.
The state is known for its significant role in the American Revolution, including events such as the Boston Tea Party and the Battle of Bunker Hill.
Harvard University, founded in 1636 in Cambridge, Massachusetts, is the oldest institution of higher education in the United States.
Massachusetts has a strong economy driven by finance, education, biotechnology, and tourism.
Cape Cod is a popular vacation destination in Massachusetts, known for its beaches and quaint towns.
The Massachusetts State House, with its distinctive golden dome, is located on Beacon Hill in Boston.
